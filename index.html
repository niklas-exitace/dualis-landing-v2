Details

Gentle Daily Shampoo

For regular wash days

A low-lather, pH-balanced formula that cleans without stripping. Removes daily dirt, oil, and sweat while preserving your hair's natural moisture barrier — the thing that keeps strands flexible, strong, and resistant to breakage.

No sulfates. No silicones. No damage-and-repair cycle.

Your hair stays hydrated between washes so it bends instead of snaps.

Size: 8 fl oz (236ml) · Supply: ~2 months (washing 3x per week) · Use: Every regular wash day

Clarifying Reset Shampoo

For your weekly deep clean

A targeted clarifying formula that removes what gentle shampoos leave behind — product buildup, hard water minerals, sebum plugs, environmental residue. The stuff that clogs follicles, suffocates your scalp, and quietly sabotages your growth.

One wash per week unclogs, resets, and lets your scalp breathe.

Clean follicles = stronger roots = less fallout = more length retained.

Size: 4 fl oz (118ml) · Supply: ~2 months (using 1x per week) · Use: Once weekly

How to Use

Days 1-6: Gentle Daily Shampoo

Wash with Gentle Daily Shampoo as needed. Skip conditioner — you won't need it.

Day 7: Clarifying Reset

Replace your regular wash with Clarifying Reset Shampoo. Massage into scalp for 60 seconds. Rinse thoroughly.

Repeat weekly. That's the entire method. No ten-step routine. No product layering. No conditioner unless your ends need extra help during the transition.
